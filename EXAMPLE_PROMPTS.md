# Coffee Industry — Example Agent Prompts

Realistic prompts that trigger the coffee-industry skill. Use these as templates.

---

## Sourcing & Origin

**"I want to source a natural process Ethiopian coffee with blueberry notes for a seasonal release. What should I look for in the contract?"**

**"Compare Ethiopian Yirgacheffe vs. Sidamo. Which has better floral characteristics and what varieties are typically grown there?"**

**"I'm opening a new cafe in Q3. What green coffee suppliers should I contact and how far in advance should I order?"**

---

## Roasting

**"I have a washed Colombian with high brightness. What roast profile would bring out its caramel notes without losing the citrus acidity?"**

**"What's the ideal DTR for a medium espresso roast? I'm seeing some baked flavors in my last batch."**

**"My roasting machine is a 12kg drum. What's the charge weight and how do I calculate a proper batch load?"**

**"After roasting, how long should I let the coffee rest before packaging? And does nitro-flushing actually extend shelf life?"**

---

## Cupping & QC

**"We received our first container from Guatemala. Walk me through the full SCA cupping protocol for this arrival."**

**"One of my lots scored 79.5 — just below specialty threshold. Can I still use it as a blender component?"**

**"I'm getting inconsistent shots even though I'm using the same beans. What QC steps should I run?"**

---

## Cafe Operations

**"My barista is pulling 42g yield from 18g dose in 22 seconds and it tastes sour. What's the fix?"**

**"I want to introduce a single-origin espresso program. How do I train my team on dial-in without wasting too much coffee?"**

**"What milk alternative works best for latte art — oat, almond, or soy? I'm trying to balance foam stability and flavor."**

---

## Supply Chain & Import

**"I want to import a container of green coffee from Colombia. Walk me through the full cost breakdown: FOB, freight, customs, warehouse."**

**"What's the difference between a fixed price contract and a pool price contract? Which is better for a small roaster?"**

**"Lead time from Colombia to US West Coast is usually how long? And what's the realistic window for sample orders?"**

---

## Pricing & Contracts

**"How do I price a 12oz bag of single-origin light roast at retail? Walk me through the full COGS calculation."**

**"I want to lock in a fixed price for 6 months. What premium should I expect to pay above C-market?"**

**"We're scaling to 500 bags/month. Should I be buying forward contracts or spot? How much buffer do I need?"**

---

## E-Commerce

**"I'm launching a roast-to-order subscription. What's a realistic subscription price point for 2 bags/month delivered?"**

**"My online coffee store has a 4% monthly churn. What are the highest-leverage retention plays?"**

**"Should I offer pre-ground or whole bean? What percentage of DTC customers buy pre-ground?"**

---

## Regulatory

**"What FDA requirements do I need to meet as a coffee importer? Do I need a food facility registration?"**

**"I'm importing from Kenya. What documentation do I need for USDA/APHIS clearance?"**

**"Can I ship roasted coffee internationally? What are the restrictions on shipping pre-packaged coffee across borders?"**

---

*These prompts are realistic use cases from specialty coffee businesses. Real agent queries should be framed similarly — specific context, clear ask.*
